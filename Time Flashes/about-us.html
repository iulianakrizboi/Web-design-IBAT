Time Flashes

There is one thing the photograph must contain,
the humanity of the moment.

Our mission is to create timeless memories focusing
on image excellence and incredible customer service.


We are continuously inspired by people we photograph
and by their life's journeys.


We go above and beyond for our clients to make sure
their time is valued, deadlines are met and the highest
quality product is delivered.

Our creative studio was formed in 2012 by two passionate and dedicated photographers with an impressive worldwide experience.



Through photographs we weave together a narrative of what is most treasured and true:
the fondness found in exchanged glances,
the giddy excitement of a bride-to-be,
the delicate peace of a newborn as it sleeps.

Based in Dublin, Ireland, we are available for weddings, celebrations, pregnancy photos,
lifestyle portrait sessions of families and children,
newborn photos and much more.
